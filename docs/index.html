Features

Everything You Need to Stay Focused

Powerful features designed to boost your productivity and help you build lasting focus habits.

⏱️

Pomodoro Timer

Quick timer presets (15, 25, 45, 60 min) with customizable work and break durations. Visual circular progress keeps you motivated.

✅

Task Management

Create tasks with descriptions, estimated durations, and track completed pomodoros. Filter by category, priority, or status.

📊

Statistics

Track daily focus time, sessions completed, and tasks finished. Weekly reports with beautiful charts and insights.

🎧

Ambient Sounds

Choose from Rain, Coffee Shop, Forest, Ocean, or White Noise to create your perfect focus environment.

🔥

Streak Tracking

Build focus habits with daily streak tracking. See your current streak and longest streak to stay motivated.

🎯

Daily Goals

Set your daily focus time goal and track progress with a beautiful progress bar. Celebrate when you achieve it!
